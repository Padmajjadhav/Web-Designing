Online Learning Websites

1.Coursera

Coursera is a leading online learning platform that partners with top universities and organizations worldwide to offer a wide range of courses, certifications, and degrees. Founded in 2012 by Stanford University computer science professors Andrew Ng and Daphne Koller, Coursera has become a popular choice for learners seeking to acquire new skills, advance their careers, or explore new subjects.

Coursera offers courses across various fields, including computer science, data science, business, humanities, and more. These courses are developed by renowned universities and organizations like Stanford, Yale, Google, and IBM. Learners can study at their own pace and on their own schedule, and courses often include videos, quizzes, assignments, and peer-reviewed projects to enhance learning.

In addition to individual courses, Coursera also offers specialized programs that consist of multiple related courses. These programs can lead to industry-recognized certificates or even degrees. Coursera's pricing is generally affordable, with many courses offered at a reasonable cost and some even being free.

Whether you're a professional looking to upskill or reskill, a student wanting to supplement your formal education, or a lifelong learner curious about various topics, Coursera offers a diverse range of opportunities to expand your knowledge and skills.
